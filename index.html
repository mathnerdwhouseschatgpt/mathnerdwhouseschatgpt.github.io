How to play

  • Use the arrow keys, WASD, or the on-screen buttons to turn.
  • Eat each snack to grow longer, earn points, and build your combo.
  • Catch a bonus star before it vanishes to trigger 2x Frenzy scoring.
  • Press Space to pause or resume the game.
